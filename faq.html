Frequently Asked Questions

What is Devs Club? ▼
Devs Club is a community of developers who come together to learn, share, and collaborate on tech projects.
What skills are needed to join the club? ▼
Basic knowledge of programming is a plus, but all levels are welcome! The club encourages continuous learning in areas like web development, data science, and app development.
How can I join the club? ▼
To join, simply sign up on our website, and you'll be invited to attend our next orientation session!
